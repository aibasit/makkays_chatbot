**Category:** Data Centers > Smart Micro Data Cabinets (MDC)  
**Capacity range:** 3-10KVA  
**Product line:** i-connect  

**Summary:**  
The i-Connect T-8001 Smart Cabinet system represents a cutting-edge solution tailored for small-scale data centers, catering predominantly to sectors like finance, government, education, healthcare, and small-scale enterprises. This intelligent single-cabinet setup encompasses a comprehensive array of functionalities, including intelligent power backup, distribution systems, environmental controls such as temperature, humidity, and smoke sensors, as well as meticulous monitoring and precision cooling mechanisms

The plug-and-play design seamlessly integrates essential components such as a rack-mounted Uninterrupted Power Supply (UPS) with a capacity of up to 10kVA, coupled with a BAT pack, a Power Distribution Unit (PDU), and an internally mounted cooling system at its base.
